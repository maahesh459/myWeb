Importance of Accessibility

Accessibility in Web-development in this day and age is not a peripheral anymore. Many organizations across the globe are discovering the importance of accommodating their customers on their websites. For instance, Did You Know? About 4.4 million Canadians (14.3%) have reported having a disability. The percentage of Canadians with disabilities increases with age, ranging from 3.7% for children 14 years and under to 56.3% for those 75 years and over. It is important to note that the spending capacity of these customers is not only significant. Every year the disabled population of Canada spends more than 25 billion dollars. The secondary reason for businesses to adopt accessibility features on websites is that Govt programs making it mandatory to provide services to the disabled.
